About

Nice to meet you

My name is Nabeel Hussain, and I am a computer science student at University of Maryland, University College. I currently work for a startup company based out of College Park, MD called TalkLocal, where I am the Operations Manager for the customer service team. I am a self taught web designer and constantly try to learn new things everyday with the hopes of becoming a full stack web developer in the near future. I also hold a degree in Economics from the University of Maryland, College Park where I graduated back in 2012.
